#### Convert the following calls of `atoi`, `atol` and `atoll` into calls of `strtol`, `strtol`, and `strtoll`, respectively.

> (a) `atoi(str)`  
> (b) `atol(str)`  
> (c) `atoll(str)`  

---

#### Answer:

> (a) `strol(str, &str, 10)`  
> (b) `strtol(str, &str, 10)`  
> (c) `strtoll(str, &str, 10)`
